Pizza Recipe

How to make pizza on a grill!

Follow this recipe for making a pizza on the grill. It tastes great and is easy to make!

Ingredients

  • 1 ready made pizza crust
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup chopped green bell pepper
  • 1/2 cup fresh sliced mushroom

Steps

  1. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oil grate.
  2. Roll out prepared pizza dough to a size that will fit your grill.
  3. Place on grill for 5 minutes, or to desired doneness, and flip over.
  4. Now add the sauce, cheese, green bell pepper and mushrooms.
  5. Cover the grill and allow to cook over high heat for 5 to 10 minutes, or until cheese is melted and bubbly.
